Rush Enterprises.
More than a Tradition of Truck Sales.

A family dealership founded in 1965, Rush Enterprises has grown to over 120 dealerships across twenty states. With over 7,500 employees and $5.5 billion in revenue, we're the largest commercial vehicle dealer group in North America. But we're a lot more than that.
