Rush Enterprises. More than a Tradition of Truck Sales.

A family dealership founded in 1965, Rush Enterprises has grown to over 120 dealerships across twenty states. With over 7,500 employees and $5.5 bliion in revenue, we're the largest commercial vehicle dealer group in North America. But we're a lot more than that.

"Only a man who knows what it is like to be defeated can reach down to the bottom of his soul and come up with the extra ounce of power it takes to win when the match is even.

- W.M. "Rusty" Rush

Expanding our reach.

We offer a variety of solutions that keep your truck on the road and you on the job.

Driven to serve.

We're in the business of serving people, and we bring an unmatched pride and accountability to work every day.
